Our Products

We have diversified into various fields and are satisfying more customers. These fields can be broadly grouped into four groups.
(1)   Electronic components

We're good in sourcing of following brands - Texas Instruments (TI) , NXP , Onsemi , FSC , Atmel , ST and Vishay.

(2)  Ultrasonic sensors

We're the distributor in India for the entire range of ultrasonic sensors from Maxbotix Inc. (USA). These sensors find application in various industrial segments.

(3)  UPS & Inverters

We supply Inverters and solar-inverters under our brand KANTEL, as well as from leading brands - Numeric , Microtek, Luminous and Su-Kam.

(4)  Digital Water Meters

Such water meters are ideally suitable for monitoring and measuring the water flow in individual flats of High-rise buildings (HRBs) , where usage of traditional water meters are not quite suitable.
